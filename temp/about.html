Wall Mounted Infrared Temperature Scanner

First of all, allow us to thank you for using our product and services. It is an honor, but also a great pleasure for us to present to you our work. On this occasion let us present our vision and mission for creating this program. Our main Vision is to prevent transmission of corona disease by checking our customer temperature and record their location history. Also, help the government to observe the spread of coronaviruses in their country

And our mission is to give accurate temperature checking results, save the customer time by using this self-services temperature scanner, minimize the chance of coronaviruses spread by reducing contact with other people, and make people more accustomed to technology..
